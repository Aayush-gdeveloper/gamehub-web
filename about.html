About Open World Reality

An "open world reality game" refers to a type of augmented reality (AR) or virtual reality (VR) experience where players are given a large, freely explorable virtual environment with the ability to interact with objects, complete objectives, and make choices with a high level of freedom, essentially simulating a real-life open world where they can roam and engage with their surroundings without strict linear progression, similar to popular open-world video games like "Grand Theft Auto" or "The Elder Scrolls."
